About Project

The aim of this project is to create a database driven web application that takes into account all the general information of kinases, Inhibitors and Phosphorylation sites. In addition users can also upload phosphoproteomic data to visualize in a graphical representation. The website was developed by a group of five students part of MSc Bioinformatics from Queen Mary's University of London, under the supervision of Dr. Conrad Bessant and Dr. Fabrizio Smeraldi.
